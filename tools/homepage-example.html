Beeld & Geluid PeerTube

Beeld & Geluid PeerTube is an open media platform that offers online access to audiovisual archive material to stimulate creative reuse. Footage from audiovisual collections can be downloaded and remixed into new works. This platform also provides an API, making it easy to develop auxiliary tools.

Access to the material on this platform is provided under the Creative Commons licensing model. Creative Commons gives authors, artists, scientists and teachers the freedom to approach their copyright in a more flexible manner and make their work available in a way they can choose themselves.

This project aims to extend PeerTube to support the availability, accessibility, and discoverability of large-scale public media collections on the next generation internet. https://beeldengeluid.github.io/extending-peertube/
